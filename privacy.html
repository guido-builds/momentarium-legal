The Momentarium

Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026

1.Scope

The Momentarium is a personal desktop application used by its owner to upload the owner's own videos to the owner's own social media accounts, including TikTok. It has no public users, no accounts, no website tracking, and no analytics.

2.What is collected

The only data the application handles is the OAuth access and refresh tokens issued when the operator authorizes their own TikTok account, together with the basic profile information (such as display name and open ID) that TikTok returns with the authorization. No data about any other person is collected or processed.

3.How it is stored and used

Tokens are stored locally on the operator's own computer and are used solely to upload the operator's videos to the operator's account through TikTok's official Content Posting API. They are never transmitted anywhere except to TikTok's API endpoints, never sold, and never shared with any third party.

4.Data retention and deletion

Tokens remain on the operator's machine until deleted. Authorization can be revoked at any time from the TikTok app (Settings, Security, Manage app permissions), which invalidates the tokens immediately; the local files can then be deleted by the operator.

5.Children

The application is not directed at children and has no users other than its adult operator.

6.Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The current version is always available at this URL.
